Horses have been around for around 55 millions years. They have evolved during that time from a small multi-toed creature (Eohippus) to the large, single-toed
animal of today. Humans started domesticating these animals in 4000BC and it became very popular in 3000BC. Over the years, many different breeds of horse have been established.

There are lots of horse breeds. Each horse breed has something special about it.
Some horse breeds look absolutly stunning. Let's take a look at the top ten horse
breeds in the world!

1. Thoroughbred

Thoroughbreds are known for racing. They are trained to race and reach top speeds but some are taught to
be used for dressage or jumping.

2. American Quarter Horse

American Quarter Horses are mainly used for Western Riding or for Racing.

3. Arabian

Arabians can be seen doing many events such as Show Jumping, Dressage and Indurance Racing.

4. Andalusian

Andalusians are mainlu used for Bull Fighting or Driving

5. Clydesdale

Since Clydesdales are big and heavy, they are using for driving wagons and carts. Since
they are mainly used for Driving, they can be seen at parks for tourists.

6. Appaloosa

Just like Arabians, Appaloosas compete in many different events.

7. Shire Horse

Shire Horses are very similar to Clydesdales exept they can also be used for
farm work such as ploughing the fields.

8. Friesian

Since Friesians are known for being very beautiful, they are mainly used in DRessage and Driving.
Some do jumping but it is more common to see them at dressage
shows.

9. Cob

Cobs are very common in England as they are very well behaved for lessons.

10. Morgan

The Morgan Horse is used for eventing.
